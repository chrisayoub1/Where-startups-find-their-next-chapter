How to Buy and Sell Startups Online in 2025

The startup acquisition market has never been more accessible. In 2025, thousands of indie founders and solopreneurs are buying and selling online businesses — from SaaS tools to newsletters, side projects to full-stack apps — without brokers, without lawyers.

StartupFlip is a free marketplace where you can list your startup for sale in minutes. Buyers browse, filter by type and price, and contact you directly on Twitter, Telegram, Instagram or email. No commissions. No broker fees.

Built by Fikrago — a blog and digital tools platform for creators. Explore our AI tools and digital products.

❓ How do I sell my startup on StartupFlip?
Click "List Your Startup", fill in your revenue, asking price, and contact details. Your listing goes live instantly — free, no approval needed.
❓ Is listing really free?
Yes. 100% free to list. Buyers reach you directly. We take zero commission. We're supported by donations and the Fikrago blog.
❓ What can I sell here?
SaaS apps, mobile apps, newsletters, e-commerce stores, web tools, agencies, side projects — any online business with revenue or traffic.
❓ What is an ARR multiple?
Asking Price ÷ Annual Revenue. A 3x means you're asking 3× the annual revenue. Most small online businesses sell at 2x–5x ARR.

About StartupFlip

StartupFlip is a free, no-commission marketplace for buying and selling startups, SaaS apps, newsletters, and online businesses. It was built by Fikrago — run by Ayoub Zinani, focused on helping people build real income online with AI tools, digital products, and smart automation.

The idea is simple: founders deserve a direct path to buyers. Most platforms charge 5–15% broker fees and take months. StartupFlip removes all of that. You list, buyers find you, you talk directly, you close on your terms.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Platform: StartupFlip by Fikrago

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a listing, you voluntarily provide: business name, description, financial figures, your name, email, and social handles. This data is stored in your browser (localStorage) and is visible to all visitors. We do not operate a server-side database.

If you subscribe via the email form (Mailchimp), your email is sent to Mailchimp under their Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Data

Listing data is displayed publicly so buyers can find and contact sellers. Email subscribers receive occasional newsletters about new listings and Fikrago content. We do not sell your data.

3. Cookies

We use localStorage to store listing data in your browser. No tracking cookies by StartupFlip. Third-party scripts (Google Fonts, Mailchimp) may set their own cookies.

4. Data Removal

Clearing browser localStorage removes listings. To remove a listing from a shared version, contact @ayoubchris8 on Telegram.

5. Financial Disclaimer

All financial figures are self-reported by sellers. StartupFlip does not verify revenue, profit, traffic, or valuation claims. Always conduct your own due diligence before purchasing. We are not liable for any losses from transactions.
